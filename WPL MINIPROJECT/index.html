About the Platform

Why EcoTrack matters

Improper waste disposal creates pollution, public health risks, and unnecessary landfill burden. EcoTrack spreads awareness about segregation, responsible disposal, and recycling so users can make better everyday environmental decisions.

Core Features

What the website offers

Waste Awareness

Learn the difference between wet, dry, plastic, hazardous, and e-waste with simple explanations.

Catalogue Search

Search common waste categories and view short disposal tips for each one.

Smart Tracker

Enter waste values in kilograms and get a suggestion based on the waste type you produce most.

Contact & Feedback

Share suggestions, doubts, or ideas through a simple contact form.

Suggested Unit

Use kilograms (kg) while tracking waste so your values stay consistent.

Best Practice

Segregate waste at source with separate bins for wet, dry, and special waste.
